Boba

Description

Bubble tea or boba is a delicious Taiwanese drink that's sweet, chewy and fun to drink. Almost everyone loves it - but what if you can't find any shops nearby? Or if you're sceptical of the ready-made ones in stores?

Our definitive guide will tell you everything you need to know about this moreish drink and even how to make it at home.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups tea
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup syrup
  • ¼ cup ice
  • ½ cup tapioca pearls

Steps

  1. Boil water to around 210 degrees Fahrenheit (98 degrees Celsius). Once the water has reached the desired temperature, pour it into the vessel with leaves or into the pot.
  2. Let it steep for five minutes before straining the tea bags or leaves. It is now ready to drink or used to prepare bubble tea.
  3. Make your simple syrup by combining equal parts water and sugar.
  4. Boil the tapioca pearls following the instructions on the package.
  5. Add your syrup, ice, milk and tea into a glass.
  6. Add the boba, and enjoy.
